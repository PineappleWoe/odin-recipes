Homemade Miso Soup

"Make your very own Japanese miso soup from scratch. It's easy to do at home! Perfect for an appetizer or light, warming lunch."

About this Recipe

Miso Soup is the perfect warm meal for those looking for a simple, but delicious soup. Miso Soup can be used as the perfect starter, or even as an affordable main. Whether you're a university student and ever have specific dietary requirements, or just a lover of Japanese cuisine, Miso Soup is a great and simple entry point into something different.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp finely chopped Wakame
  • 2 tsp Dashi granules
  • 3 tbsp Miso Paste
  • 4 Ounces silken Tofu (cubed)
  • 2 Green onions, slice on the bias
  • 4 Cups Water

Method

  1. Place the Wakame in a fine-mesh sieve, and soak in some cold water for 10 minutes.
  2. Combine 4 Cups of Water and Dashi granules in a saucepan and bring to a boil over medium head. Add the Miso Paste and whisk until dissolved. Add Wakame and simmer for 3 minutes.
  3. Divide Tofu between 4 serving bowls. Ladle Miso Soup on top and garnish with Green Onions.
